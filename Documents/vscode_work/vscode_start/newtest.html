some notable facts of the life of this great man are;

  • He created the linux kernel at the age of 20
  • He worked at Transmeta Corp for six years from 1997
  • He worked at Open Source Development Labs (OSDL) as a project coordinator
  • He among the early founder of the linux foundation
  • He made linux open source under the GPL licensing in the year 1992
  • He is the head of the linux kernel, every patches by every developer require is approval
  • He was awarded the Millennium Technology Prize by the foundation Technology Academy Finland in 2012
  • He is the creator of the version control system call Git
  • He is a recipient of the 2014 IEEE Computer Society Computer Pioneer Award
  • He is a recipient of the 2018 IEEE Masaru Ibuka Consumer Electronics Award.
